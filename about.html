Mission Statement

Kittes To Go was founded with the intent of empowering kittens. We want our clients to feel at home, while knowing that they are contributing something great to our community and society at large. Our main goal is to create an environment where kitties can afford the ever increasing milk and tuna prices, while providing a soothing experience for our customers.
